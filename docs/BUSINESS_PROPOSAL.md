### 1.2 Regulatory Landscape

#### Key Jurisdictions

**Malta (EU Gateway)**
- VFA Framework under Malta Financial Services Authority (MFSA)
- License Types: VFA Agent, VFA Exchange, VFA Service Provider
- Compliance Requirements: MiCA Regulation alignment
- Estimated Timeline: 6-9 months
- Cost: €50,000 - €150,000

**Cyprus (EU Alternative)**
- CySEC Regulation for Investment Firms
- AIFM License for fund management
- Passporting rights across EU
- Timeline: 8-12 months
- Cost: €75,000 - €200,000

**Dubai (MENA Hub)**
- VARA (Virtual Asset Regulatory Authority) oversight
- DFSA (Dubai Financial Services Authority) for finance
- Free Zone advantages (DIFC, ADGM)
- Timeline: 4-6 months
- Cost: $100,000 - $250,000

**Singapore (Asia-Pacific Gateway)**
- MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore) DPT License
- CMS (Capital Markets Services) License
- Largest crypto hub in Asia-Pacific
- Timeline: 6-12 months
- Cost: S$150,000 - S$400,000

**Switzerland (Zug - Crypto Valley)**
- FINMA oversight for financial services
- Banking license or securities dealer license
- Established crypto-friendly framework
- Timeline: 9-15 months
- Cost: CHF 200,000 - CHF 500,000

**South Korea (Seoul)**
- FSC (Financial Services Commission) approval
- VASP (Virtual Asset Service Provider) registration
- Strict AML/KYC requirements
- Timeline: 6-9 months
- Cost: ₩200M - ₩500M

**United Kingdom (London)**
- FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) registration
- Electronic Money Institution (EMI) or Payment Institution license
- Post-Brexit framework
- Timeline: 12-18 months
- Cost: £150,000 - £500,000
